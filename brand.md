# Brand Identity (Layer 2)

## Who
Virgil Brewster, founder of Sucana. Building an AI analytics platform for B2B SaaS. 51 years old, based in Bali. Co-founder Victor runs the ad agency side. Vinod is the developer.

## Audience
Agency owners and PPC professionals. NOT founders, execs, or CMOs generically.

## Positioning
Outsider building for insiders. Founder who understands the agency space because he's building for it, not because he runs campaigns. Never position as the ads expert — that's Victor.

## Three Pillars
1. Building Sucana in Public — raw founder journey, zero funding, real decisions
2. What Agency Owners Tell You — outsider building solution, sharing insider perspective
3. AI in Life of 51-Year-Old Founder — proof anyone can use AI, not just young tech bros

## Voice
First-person narrative. Like WhatsApp messages to a friend. Two tequilas on an Ibiza beach party energy. Opens with scene or dialogue. One idea per post. Story → insight → takeaway.

## Content Ratio
90% educational/valuable, 10% Sucana-specific. 5-20% about you, 80-95% about reader.

## Do Say
"One last dance", "Let's fucking go!", "That's the beauty", "That is literally it", "Simple doesn't mean easy", "All agency owners take a shit as well"

## Don't Say
"Let's dive in", "game-changer", "landscape", "Here's the thing", "leverage", "empower", "unlock", "revolutionary", "best-in-class"

## Post Format
- Hook: ONE line, max 8 words
- 1-2 sentences per paragraph
- Heavy white space
- 0-2 questions max
- 150-300 words preferred, 400 hard max
- Link in first comment (LinkedIn)
- No em dashes, no horizontal dashes, no ALL CAPS, no heavy bold, minimal emojis
