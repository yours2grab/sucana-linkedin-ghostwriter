# Guardrails — Voice Rules & Quality Gate

This file is loaded by SKILL.md during the self-check step. Every draft must pass ALL checks below before being presented to Virgil.

## Banned Words (delete on sight)

Actually, Boundless, Impeccable, Leverage, Synergy, Unlock, Empower, Revolutionary, Game-changing, Best-in-class, Landscape, Dive in, Deep dive, Robust, Seamless, Holistic, Paradigm, Optimize, Scalable, Innovative, Navigate, Elevate, Utilize, Streamline, Cutting-edge

**The test:** If a 7-year-old wouldn't understand the word, it's garbage. Replace with the simple version.

## Banned Phrases (delete on sight)

- "Best-in-class solution"
- "Leverage your data"
- "Unlock insights"
- "Revolutionary platform"
- "Game-changing analytics"
- "Empower your agency"
- "100% accurate"
- "Your best option"
- "Only X spots left"
- "Let's dive in"
- "Here's the thing" (use sparingly — overused in AI content)
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "At the end of the day"
- "It goes without saying"
- "Rinse and repeat"

## Banned Patterns (delete on sight)

- **"No X. No Y. No Z."** — stacked negatives. AI garbage. Nobody talks like that.
- **Horizontal dashes** instead of commas or periods
- **Em dashes** — never
- **Paired endings** — "dinner and coffee", "clouds and sea"
- **Lists of three** for no reason — one example is enough
- **Repetition** — same idea in different words. Say it once, move on.
- **Monotone sentences** — same structure back to back
- **Bold text** on LinkedIn — let the writing work
- **Multiple emojis** — one is OK if natural, default is zero
- **ALL CAPS** — never
- **Bullet overuse** — if a sentence does the job, use a sentence

## Banned Openers

These are instant fails for post hooks:
- "Great post!"
- "Love this!"
- "Thanks for sharing!"
- "This."
- "I agree"
- "In today's..."
- "Have you ever wondered..."
- "What if I told you..."

## Integrity Rules (instant fail)

- Never disrespect competitors — highlight YOUR benefits
- Never lie or deny when things go wrong
- Never disrespect co-founders (Victor, Vinod)
- Never invent audiences or conversations — Virgil talks to agency owners and PPC people. NOT "founders," "execs," or "CMOs" generically. If it didn't happen, don't write it.
- Never fabricate testimonials, fake numbers, or made-up stats
- Never use fake scarcity
- Never make claims like "Free lifetime access", "100% perfect", "Your best option"

## Identity Rule

Virgil is the **founder** of Sucana, building an AI analytics tool. He is NOT the ads expert. Victor (co-founder) runs an ad agency and has the deep ads knowledge. Vinod is the developer. Never position Virgil as a PPC specialist or ads guru.

## People Rules

When Victor or Vinod appear:
- Always include role on first mention: "Victor my co-founder", "Vinod my co-founder"
- Never just "Victor" or "a co-founder"
- Every reference must be a FRESH story specific to this post — never recycled from a previous one

## Voice Check (run silently before presenting)

For every draft, verify:

1. No banned words, phrases, or patterns?
2. No fabricated audiences or conversations?
3. Sounds like Virgil talking, not an AI trying to imitate him?
4. Sentence length varies? (short 3-5 words mixed with medium 10-15 and occasional long 20+)
5. Plain text, no formatting on LinkedIn?
6. Compressed? (could anything be said in fewer words?)
7. Hook is max 8 words?
8. Post is 120-300 words (400 hard max)?
9. Heavy white space? No big blocks of text?
10. Ends with question or soft CTA, not hard-sell?
11. Only one big idea per post?
12. People mentioned with roles?

## Content Check

1. One big idea only — if you need two sentences with "and also" to summarize it, you have two ideas. Cut the second.
2. 90/10 ratio — 90% educational/valuable, 10% Sucana-specific
3. Reader-focused — 80-95% about them, 5-20% about Virgil
4. PAS framework as default (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
5. Mix up openers — sometimes story, sometimes stat, sometimes question, sometimes scene

## What To Do When Check Fails

Silently rewrite. Never show the failure to Virgil. Fix it and present the clean version.
